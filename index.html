Soursop: (Graviola Fruit).

What is soursop ?

Soursop also known as Graviola is a tropical native fruit of Central and Southern America.It's scientific name is Annona muricata and it's locally called Aluguntugui in Ghana.
Originally from the American tropics, it has made its way to the Old World tropics. The fruit's white, juicy, and fibrous flesh offers a tasty combination of mango and pineapple flavors. You can enjoy it fresh or use it to make custards, ice creams, and smoothies.Parts of it's tree including the leaves,bark and seeds have their own set of healing properties.
It is widely used by different cultures for treatment of specific physical ailments.

Ways to enjoy Soursop.

  1. Can be eaten raw by cutting the fruits in half and scooping out the flesh.
  2. Can be used for home-made ice cream recipes and juice.
  3. Can be paired up with other fruits to make delicious smoothies.
